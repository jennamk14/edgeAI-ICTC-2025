Workshop Format & Activities

Draft schedule — subject to updates.


Session 1: Edge AI in Action Keynote Talk

  • Welcome and context: why edge AI matters for conservation
  • Keynote talk followed by Q&A

Break


Session 2: ICICLE Infrastructure and Tools Deep Dive (60 min)

  • ICICLE cyberinfrastructure for animal ecology: resources, data platforms, tools
  • Access paths, integration between edge devices and cloud resources
  • Technical showcase of leading edge AI hardware/software
  • Demo station rotation: hands-on with edge AI systems

Lunch Break


Session 3: Panel Discussion

  • Experts from academia, industry, and conservation practice discuss:
    • Deployment strategies for resource-limited settings
    • Ethical considerations and governance of edge AI in conservation
    • Future directions and community needs
    • Q&A on common challenges and cross-platform opportunities

Break


Session 4: Community Demos & Knowledge Exchange

  • Bioacoustics — deploying edge AI for sound-based monitoring
  • Camera traps — training and deploying vision models on edge devices
  • Aerial systems — edge AI for drone-based conservation
  • Getting started — edge AI fundamentals for beginners
  • Community-led sessions and breakout troubleshooting

Break


Session 4: WILDLABS Edge Computing Community Launch

  • Vision, mission, and governance
  • Working group formation: domain, cross-cutting themes, regional contexts
  • Collaborative action planning and next steps beyond ICTC

Poster Session & Networking Mixer(Tentative)

  • Poster and demo presentations from community members
  • Networking and discussion
