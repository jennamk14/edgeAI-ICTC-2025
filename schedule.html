Workshop Format & Activities

Draft schedule — subject to updates.

Featured speaker: Dante Wasmuht — Head of AI/ML at Conservation X Labs & Director, Sentinel Program.

Session 1: Edge AI in Action — Speed Showcase (120 min + 15 min intermission)

  • Welcome and context: why edge AI matters for conservation
  • Hackster.io Edge AI Earth Guardians Contest highlights
  • Lightning demos (tentative): bioacoustics, camera traps, drones, Microsoft SPARROW
  • Q&A on common challenges and cross-platform opportunities

Break (30 min)

Session 2: ICICLE Infrastructure and Tools Deep Dive (60 min)

  • ICICLE cyberinfrastructure for animal ecology: resources, data platforms, tools
  • Access paths, integration between edge devices and cloud resources
  • Technical showcase of leading edge AI hardware/software
  • Demo station rotation: hands-on with edge AI systems

Lunch (90 min)

Session 3: Community Tutorials & Knowledge Exchange (120 min + 15 min intermission)

  • Track A: Bioacoustics — deploying edge AI for sound-based monitoring
  • Track B: Camera traps — training and deploying vision models on edge devices
  • Break (15 min)
  • Track A: Aerial systems — edge AI for drone-based conservation
  • Track B: Getting started — edge AI fundamentals for beginners
  • Community-led sessions and breakout troubleshooting

Break (30 min)

Session 4: WILDLABS Edge Computing Community Launch (90 min)

  • Vision, mission, and governance
  • Working group formation: domain, cross-cutting themes, regional contexts
  • Collaborative action planning and next steps beyond ICTC
